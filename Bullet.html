Angela's Recipe for the Best Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough

  • 3/4 Cup warm milk
  • 2 1/4 teaspoons yeast
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 3 Cups bread flour

For the Fillings:

  • 2/3 Cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, suga
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. knead the dough for 10 min
